Meet Lottie!

In December 2018, I had come home from spending the weekend with my grandparents and when I arrived home, there was Lottie, the 12 week old mini schnazuer and in my Mum's arms. Although she was as shy as anything, the next day My dad and I began training her ourselves. No puppy school. Just two first time dog-owners with a bag of treats, and a dog who just wants to play. But only a couple months after adopting Lottie, she soon got quite sick, no one was sure why and there were a few nights where we had to leave Lottie behind at the Vet. The Vet soon figured out that Lottie gets very sick if she eats dog biscuits which is unfortunely the most common dog food, so the other option was to put her on a raw meat diet. What happened to Lottie, encouraged me to find alternatives rather than biscuits and try out pet food recipes, so when you want take a look at the pet food recipes section of my website. Now Lottie is 6 and a half, is a pretty well trained dog minus the excessive barking when someones at the door and doesn't get as sick as she used too.

Our Purpose!

Lottie + Sof wants to make Dog training easier and more accessible for you as puppy school prices are pricey, so I figured creating a website that people can use rather than paying and going to puppy school. I originally learnt the basics of dog training through a book, but lets be honest no one really reads. Looking after dog can be hard, they sometimes poop in your room, don't stop barking and bite but with Lottie + Sof we try our best to make training and looking after your dog a breeze. The pet food recipes section of my website shows you alternatives and homemade treats for you dog. I also have tried out these recipes and put to the test which recipes are best and worse.
